## Interpretation for HPCA Paper

These wafer results are from a **trace-driven wafer-scale simulator**, not real wafer hardware measurements. H100 runs are used for trace collection and cost calibration when a local model is available. The simulator exposes graph-level KV sharing, SRAM pressure, mesh traffic/hotspot, scheduling, utilization, and energy estimates so the paper can argue about system and architecture behavior rather than multi-agent answer quality alone.
